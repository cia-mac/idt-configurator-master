THE RULES
DUAL CABLE
Dual-capable cameras use 2 ports for FULL FPS. Available on processors with 2+ ports.
SINGLE-ONLY CAMERAS = ALWAYS FULL FPS
Helios, Phoenix CR, XS II always run at full frame rate regardless of connection mode.
DUAL CAMERA ON SINGLE PORT = HALF FPS
Galileo, Phoenix GOLD, Phoenix on single cable lose half their frame rate.
